Who We Are

At Neelkanth Enterprises, we are committed to revolutionizing the way people harness solar energy.
Founded with a vision of promoting sustainability and energy independence, our company specializes in
providing high-quality solar energy solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. With a
strong focus on innovation, efficiency, and affordability, we strive to make clean energy accessible to
everyone.

Established on April 17, 2023, Neelkanth Enterprises has grown under the leadership of our dedicated team.
Our Owner, Ranjit Kumar Singh, along with Co-owners Rounit Raj and Rajnish Raj, brings
extensive experience and expertise in the renewable energy sector. Together, we are committed to
delivering cutting-edge solar solutions that not only reduce electricity costs but also contribute to a
greener and more sustainable future.

At Neelkanth Enterprises, we believe in excellence, customer satisfaction, and environmental
responsibility. Our goal is to empower individuals and businesses with reliable solar solutions, helping
them transition to a cleaner and more energy-efficient way of living.

Our Mission

To empower communities by providing clean, renewable, and efficient energy solutions.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in sustainable energy solutions, driving a greener future for generations.
