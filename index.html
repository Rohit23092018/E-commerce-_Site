About Us

At Neelkanth Enterprises, we are dedicated to promoting sustainable and efficient solar energy solutions. Since our GST registration on April 17, 2023, we have been committed to providing high-quality solar services.

Led by our Owner, Ranjit Kumar Singh, along with Co-owners Rounit Raj and Rajnish Raj, our mission is to make renewable energy accessible to all. We strive to empower businesses and households with clean energy while reducing environmental impact.
